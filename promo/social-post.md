# PicLet - Professional Image Tools in Your Browser

**Edit. Scale. Ship.**

Transform your images without uploading them anywhere. PicLet runs 100% in your browser - your files never leave your device.

**Features:**
- Remove backgrounds with smart edge detection
- Generate app icons (ICO, Android, iOS, Web)
- Create store assets for Unity, Steam & more
- Color accent effect (keep one color, grayscale rest)
- Crop & scale with aspect ratio presets
- Batch download as ZIP

**Privacy-first. No servers. No subscriptions.**

Try it free: https://piclet.app
